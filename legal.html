2. Privacy Policy

This policy describes how 4SP collects, uses, and protects your personal and non-personal data.

Information We Collect

  • Account Data: We collect your email address, unique user ID (UID), and basic profile information when you authenticate using our Firebase system. This includes sign-in methods like Google, GitHub, and Microsoft.
  • Notes Content: All text entered into the Notes application is encrypted client-side using the Crypto-JS library before it is stored. We do not have the ability to decrypt or view the plaintext content of your notes.
  • Messenger Content: Messages and files in Messenger V2 are subject to hybrid storage methods, but your identity is associated with the content.
  • Settings: We store user preferences, such as your customized Navigation Color, in your browser's localStorage.

Humanity Agent (AI) Data

The Humanity Agent utilizes Gemini models. Your prompts and the AI's responses are processed by Google's Generative Language API.

  • Location Data: Location Sharing is OFF by default. If you enable this setting, we use your browser's geolocation to get your coordinates, which are then used by Nominatim (OpenStreetMap) to perform a reverse geocode for contextually relevant answers (e.g., local weather).
  • Web Search: The Web Search toggle controls whether the AI can perform live web searches to answer your query.

Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate the Service:

  • Firebase: For user authentication, database, and infrastructure.
  • Google / Gemini: For processing and responding to queries via the Humanity Agent.
  • Nominatim / OpenStreetMap: For reverse geocoding location data when location sharing is enabled.
  • dictionaryapi.dev: For providing word definitions through the Dictionary application.
